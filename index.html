Accessibility Hackathon

Space Telescope Science Institute

March 10, 2023

On March 10, 2023, Space Telescope Science Institute invites STEM researchers, practitioners, and community members to an Accessibility Hackathon to improve the accessibility of public resources shared by STScI and other organizations in the scientific computing community. The Hackathon, held remotely, will begin with a short kickoff event to provide goals, information, and materials to participants. During the day, individuals and small teams will contribute alt text, audio descriptions, transcripts, issues, and other materials to STScI and participating organizations. Participants in the event will not only improve accessibility in scientific and research computing materials and infrastructure, but also raise awareness of critical issues related to accessibility and STEM.
